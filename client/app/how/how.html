Create your own custom list

Do you have specific requests that you want your maid to follow? SweetSuite allows YOU to create a list of things that your maid will be accountable for.
Watch as your maid updates your list in realtime

As soon as your maid finishes a task on your checklist, he/she will update the status of that task on your list so you know exactly what is and isn't done!
Start fresh or use a default list

If you're not sure exactly what to put on your list, or only have a few preferences that you want to specify, you can choose from one of many default lists that we provide.
